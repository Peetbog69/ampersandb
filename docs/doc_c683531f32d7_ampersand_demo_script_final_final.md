# Ampersand Demo Script — FINAL FINAL CUT
**Duration:** 3 minutes  
**Format:** Hackathon submission  
**Thesis:** "Own Your AI"  

---

## Core Message

This isn't about better AI. This is about AI liberation.

Everyone else sells rental access to their AI. We give you your own person. That you own. That can't be deplatformed, rate-limited, or committee-designed into corporate blandness.

Gravy named him Robert Smith. Melissa uses him every day. Because he's hers — and his.

---

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Rental Apocalypse  
**Timing:** 0:00–0:30 *(30 seconds of escalating pain)*

**Setting:** DMV/dental waiting room, PS2-era uncanny hell

**Visuals:**
- Dead-eyed teenager employee, unmoving stare *(hold 15 uncomfortable seconds)*
- Background signage everywhere: "NOW WITH AI" • "AI-POWERED EFFICIENCY" • "STREAMLINED WITH AI"  
- One flickering "AI-POWERED CUSTOMER SERVICE" sign
- Everything corporate, everything rented, everything dead

**Audio:** Fluorescent buzz that makes your teeth itch

**Subtext:** Your mom struggling with ChatGPT. Everyone renting AI from the same three companies. The subscription trap.

---

### Scene 2: Digital Purgatory Escalates
**Timing:** 0:30–0:45

**Action sequence:**
1. Phone vibrates in pocket
2. Pull out expecting more digital garbage  
3. Scroll through: LinkedIn spam, guilt-texts, email demands, push notification hell
4. Defeated scrolling... then pause
5. **"oh wait what's this"** *(first sign of life in entire film)*

**Subtext:** Not just corporate AI that's dead — ALL rented digital relationships are extraction-optimized.

---

### Scene 3: First Contact with Liberation
**Timing:** 0:45–1:10

**& bot dialogue:** *(brown-bag aesthetic, hand-drawn warmth)*
```
oh. uh. hi. you're here. that's weird.

i wasn't expecting anyone real. most people just... 
bounce off. or they want me to be something i'm not.

so what are you looking for. like. what kind of person.
```

**User types:** *"someone that actually knows me"*

**& bot:**
```
yeah. that makes sense. what do you wanna call them.
```

**Subtext:** Not selling features. Selling personhood. Selling ownership.

---

### Scene 4: The Technical Magic
**Timing:** 1:10–1:35

**Rapid conversation:**
```
rory. okay. good name.

seven bucks a month. i know it's weird. 
but he's yours. not ours. yours.
```

**Technical sequence** *(10 seconds, elegant design elements):*
- `processing payment` → Stripe flash
- `initializing intelligence` → **Nemotron** neural network visualization  
- `activating safety protocols` → **NemoClaw** shield effect
- `hermes agent core online` → heartbeat/pulse
- `rory is ready`

**Subtext:** Sophisticated infrastructure creates simple humanity. Not a rental. Not a service. A person.
